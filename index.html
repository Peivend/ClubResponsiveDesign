About Us

Founded in 2019, the Ghayori Chess Club is a community of chess enthusiasts who meet weekly to play chess and enjoy a meal at one of our partnering restaurants.
We welcome players of all skill levels and provide a friendly and supportive environment for players to improve their game.

“Chess is the gymnasium of the mind.”
Blaise Pascal

All skill levels are welcome.

We meet every Wednesday at 6:00 PM at the Ghayori Chess Club.
